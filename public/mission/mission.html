Glass Ceiling & Co.

The mission of Glass Ceiling Company is to provide a job seeking website that caters to the needs of black women. For Black Women, Made By Black Women, but accessible to everyone.

Founded by 5 Spelman Students - Lelia Hampton, Jaida Langham, Margie Ruffin, Zari McFadden, and Ulumna Egwim, these women set out to create a website that would cater to their needs and what they look for within the job searching process during their respective senior years.
